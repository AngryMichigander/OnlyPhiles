About OnlyPhiles

OnlyPhiles is a public accountability database documenting Republican politicians, officials, and party-adjacent figures who have been accused, charged, or convicted of sexual offenses — particularly crimes against children.

This site exists because accountability requires visibility. These are public figures who sought or held positions of public trust. Their records should be searchable and accessible.

How it works

Each entry is categorized by status (convicted, charged, or alleged), political level (federal, state, local, party official, or adjacent), and crime type. Source links are included for every entry where available.

The "alleged" designation is used where charges have not been filed or a conviction has not occurred. Inclusion is based on credible news reporting.

Legal note: All persons are presumed innocent until proven guilty in a court of law. Entries marked "alleged" or "charged" reflect accusations only. Source links are provided for all entries so readers can review the underlying reporting.
